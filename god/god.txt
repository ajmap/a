Father, source of life, I acknowledge that time is in my mind and my mind is in you. I renounce anxiety, hurry and all decrees of temporal scarcity. I declare that my consciousness is at peace, that time expands, and each moment is fully sufficient because you are my source and in you all things are possible. Thank you for today. I live in the abundance of your time and I experience it as such because I decree it. The mental law never fails. Time responds to the generosity of my consciousness. I have control today. I choose to expand my time from peace. So it is.


God, I am deeply grateful that You allow me to think in English,
that You open my mind to understand, to learn, and to grow beyond limits I once knew.

Thank You for this ability to express myself, to connect with others across cultures,
to receive knowledge, wisdom, and inspiration through this language.

Strengthen my mind so that my thoughts may be clear, focused, and purposeful.
Guide my words so that what I speak and think reflects truth, kindness, and understanding.

Let this gift not be taken for granted, but used with intention—
to learn more, to help others, and to become the person You created me to be.

Fill me with confidence when I think, speak, and express myself in English,
and remove any doubt or fear that holds me back.

May my mind remain open, my heart humble, and my spirit strong,
so that everything I learn and say may serve a greater good.

God, I am deeply grateful that You allow me to think in English. Thank You for opening my mind to another language, another way of expressing thought, creativity, and understanding.

Please strengthen this ability within me. Let my thoughts flow freely and naturally in English, without hesitation, without fear, and without the need to translate back and forth. Remove the doubt and resistance that hold me back, and replace them with confidence, clarity, and ease.

Help me to trust the knowledge I already carry. Guide my mind so that English becomes a place of comfort, not effort — a space where ideas come alive effortlessly and creatively. Let the words come to me with precision and feeling, so that I can think, learn, and grow more fully through them.

Give me the courage to stay in English when I practice, even when it feels unfamiliar. Strengthen my focus so I do not fall back into old habits, but instead build new ones with patience and consistency.

May my thoughts become sharper, my imagination richer, and my expression more powerful through this language. And may I use this ability not only for myself, but to connect, understand, and contribute to the world around me.

Thank You for this gift, and for the growth that is already happening within me.
