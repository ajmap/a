I see myself on the beach of a beautiful tropical island. I feel the warmth of the sand on my bare feet. I feel the warm sand oozing between my toes as I press my feet into the soft forgiving sand. I see myself sitting in a soft luxereous lounge chair. I feel completely comfortable, completely at rest. I gaze out at the ocean, the water is calm and serene. There are gentle ocean swirls that are in rhythm with my breathing, a slow relaxing rhythm and as I breath out, I let go of tiny bits of tension that linger into my body. Each time I exhale, a little more tension melts and flows out of my body. Each time I exhale, I am more and more relaxed. I smell the fresh salt air, I feel the soft touch of the tropical breeze. In the distance I hear seagulls. I can see the seagulls swooping up and down near the shore line. I hear the whisper of the breeze passing through the fronds of the palm trees lining the beach. I watch the thin palm trees sway gently in the tropical breeze. I scoop up a fistful of sand and I let it fall back on the beach. I see how effortlessly it falls and forms a pile. There is a complete lack of tension. My eyes feast under vivid blue's and greens of the coral reefs. I am so relaxed, every muscle in my body is limp, every part of my body feels heavy and relaxed. I feel a nice warm glow throughout my body. That is the result of perfect unhindered circulation brought about by perfect relaxation. I wallow in this feeling. I relish this state of mind. I know it is a healthy state and that my body is rejuvenating itself, energizing itself, healing itself, easily and effortlessly each time I relax. Each time I go to this state, which is my sanctuary, relaxation is easier and easier. Each time I practise deep relaxation I relax faster and to a deeper healthy mental state. Each time the feelings are deeper and more enjoyable. Each time I practise deep relaxation, I reach a place where I am poised, happy, and feel healthier.
